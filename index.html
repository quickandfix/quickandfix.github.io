FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

What can you take?▸
Most household junk, furniture, appliances, bagged trash, construction debris, yard waste, garage/basement cleanouts. No hazardous chemicals.
Do you do stairs or basements?▸
Yes—basements, attics, tight hallways, and multi-floor properties. We plan crew size and equipment based on access.
Are you licensed and insured?▸
Yes. We’re fully licensed and insured for residential and commercial work.
Do you offer same-day service?▸
Usually yes. Text photos for the fastest estimate and we’ll offer the first available window.
How does pricing work?▸
Based on volume (truck space), labor, and disposal fees. You get an upfront price before any work begins.
Do I have to move anything myself?▸
Nope. You point—we lift, load, and sweep up when finished.
What payment methods do you accept?▸
Cash, Cash App, Zelle, Apple Pay, and major cards.
Do you service my area?▸
Philadelphia, Delaware County, Camden County, Bucks & Montgomery. Not sure? Ask us.
Do I need to be there for pickup?▸
For curbside/driveway pickups, no. For inside access, someone should be on-site to let us in.
What don’t you take?▸
We can’t take hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, fuels, batteries). We can point you to city disposal sites.
