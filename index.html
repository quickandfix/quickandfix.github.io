What items can you take?

Most household junk: furniture, appliances, mattresses, electronics, bagged trash, construction debris, yard waste, and more. We avoid hazardous materials (paint/chemicals) unless pre‑approved.

Do you recycle or donate?

Yes—when possible we divert reusable items to donation and recycle metal/cardboard.

Are you insured?

Yes. We’re licensed & insured for junk removal and light handyman work.
